Common Questions

Quick Answers

How long does shipping take?
Standard shipping takes 5–7 business days. Express (2–3 days) is available at checkout. Orders over $100 receive free standard shipping automatically.
Can I return or exchange an item?
Yes — we accept returns within 30 days on unworn, unwashed items with original tags attached. Final sale items are not eligible for return.
How do I know what size to order?
Each product page includes detailed sizing information and a size guide. When in doubt, reach out to us and we'll help you find the perfect fit.
Do you offer wholesale or collaborations?
We're always open to conversations about wholesale partnerships and creative collaborations. Reach out via the form above with your proposal.
