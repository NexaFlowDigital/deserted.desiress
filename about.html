The Beginning

A vision forged
in the heat

Deserted Desiress was founded with one belief: that every garment should feel like it was made just for you. Inspired by the raw, uncompromising nature of desert landscapes and the burning desire to create something different — this brand was born.

We design every piece in-house, working with premium manufacturers to ensure our vision is executed without compromise. The result is clothing that lives between worlds — sophisticated enough for luxury, rugged enough for the street.

Based in Texas, with a mindset that belongs everywhere.

How We Work

From concept
to closet.

Our production process is built on trust, precision, and an unrelenting commitment to quality. We handle every step with intention — from the first sketch to the final stitch.

Step 01
Design

Every piece begins as a hand sketch. Our creative director develops each concept with meticulous attention to silhouette, proportion, and detail.

Step 02
Sampling

Samples are produced by our manufacturing partner and reviewed multiple times. We reject and revise until the sample is exactly right.

Step 03
Production

Once approved, pieces are manufactured in limited runs. We control quantity to maintain exclusivity and reduce waste.

Step 04
Drop

Collections drop via our Shopify store and Instagram. Early access is given to our newsletter subscribers first — always.
